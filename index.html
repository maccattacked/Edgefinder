How ranking works

EdgeFinder analyses every upcoming fixture that has valid odds, compares model probability with bookmaker price, removes anything below your minimum edge, then displays the five strongest remaining bets. Football-Data commonly supplies Home, Draw, Away and Over 2.5 odds.

Elo power model

Elo ratings update after every match using result, margin, venue and opponent strength. The backtest compares this model with the existing opponent-adjusted Poisson model.

Opponent-strength engine

EdgeFinder now rates every goal against the quality of the opponent. Scoring against a strong defence raises attack strength more than scoring against a weak defence. Conceding to a strong attack is penalised less than conceding to a weak one. Ratings are recalculated repeatedly until they stabilise.
